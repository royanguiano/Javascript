Workshops for Career Development

The Office of Career Services & Leadership Development (CSLD) offers a wide variety of workshops available to all current SF State students and alumni. To register for a workshop, please check out our workshop calendar and register for the event you wish to attend by following the link and logging on to your Orgsync account. Please click on "workshops" bar on the left to view the types of professional development workshops CSLD has to offer.
